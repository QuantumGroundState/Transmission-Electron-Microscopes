# Transmission-Electron-Microscopes
TEMs around the world

# UCLA
## Electron Imaging Center for Nanosystems / Nanomachines (EICN)
### Titan Krios G1 TEM
* **Acceleration Voltage:** 300 kV
* **Electron Source:** Field Emission Gun (S-FEG)
* **Lenses:** C-TWIN lens (Cs=2.7 mm, Cc=2.7 mm)
* **Resolution:** 1.4 Å (information limit)
* **Detectors/Cameras:** Gatan image filter (GIF) BioQuantum with K3 camera
* **Stage:** Computer-controlled compustage with +/- 70º double-tilt 

### Titan 80-300 TEM
* **Acceleration Voltage:** 80, 200, 300 kV alignments
* **Electron Source:** Field Emission Gun (X-FEG)
* **Lenses:** S-TWIN lens (Cs=1.2 mm, Cc=1.4 mm)
* **Resolution:** 1.34 Å 
* **Detectors/Cameras:** Direct Electron DE64 Camera with 4DSTEM package, Gatan Ultrascan US1000 2K digital camera
* **Spectroscopy:** Oxford X-MaxN 100TLE 100 mm2 SDD for TEM (EDS)

### Talos F200C G2 TEM
* **Acceleration Voltage:** 200 kV
* **Electron Source:** Thermionic 
* **Lenses:** C-Twin pole piece gap
* **Resolution:** 1-2 nanometers
* **Detectors/Cameras:** Thermo Scientific Ceta 16M and Ceta-D CMOS cameras optimized for low-dose diffraction

### T12 TEM / Quick CryoEM (Tecnai)
* **Acceleration Voltage:** 20 to 120 kV
* **Electron Source:** LaB6 gun
* **Lenses:** TWIN lens
* **Resolution:** 0.34 nm (point-to-point), 0.2 nm (line)
* **Detectors/Cameras:** TVIPS F415MP 4k Camera or Gatan Ultrascan1000 4MP CCD

# USC
## Core Center of Excellence in Nano Imaging (CNI)
### JEOL JEM-2100F TEM
* **Electron Source:** Thermal field emission source
* **Lenses:** High-resolution pole-piece (HRP)
* **Resolution:** 230 pm in TEM mode and 160 pm in STEM mode
* **Detectors/Cameras:** Orius Pre-GIF camera, Gatan UltraScan 1000 Post-GIF camera
* **Spectroscopy:** Electron energy loss spectroscopy (Quantum SE GIF) and x-ray spectroscopy (Oxford X-MaxN 100 TLE Windowless SDD)

### Thermo Fisher Titan Krios Cryo-TEM
* **Application:** Advanced atomic-level structural biology and high-resolution cryo-EM
* **Detectors/Cameras:** K3 direct detection camera
* **Operation:** Shared access partnership between USC and Amgen

### Thermo Fisher Glacios Cryo-TEM
* **Application:** High-throughput cryo-electron microscopy
* **Detectors/Cameras:** Falcon 4 direct detection camera

### Thermo Fisher Spectra 200 STEM
* **Mode:** Scanning Transmission Electron Microscopy
* **Function:** High-resolution atomic scale imaging and microanalysis 

### Thermo Fisher Talos F200C STEM
* **Mode:** Scanning Transmission Electron Microscopy 
* **Function:** High-throughput 2D and 3D imaging and compositional analysis
